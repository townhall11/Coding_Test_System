our menu popular menu

Barako Coffee (Kapeng Barako)

A strong and bold coffee variety made from Liberica beans, primarily grown in Batangas and Cavite.Known for its intense aroma, smoky, and slightly fruity flavor.Often served black or with muscovado sugar.

₱20.00

Benguet Coffee

A type of Arabica coffee grown in the highlands of Benguet, Cordillera region.Has a smooth, mildly sweet, and nutty flavor with hints of chocolate.One of the most sought-after premium coffees in the country.

₱30.00

Sagada Coffee

Another Arabica coffee variety grown in Sagada, Mountain Province.Known for its fruity, floral notes, and balanced acidity.Often compared to Benguet coffee but has a slightly more complex taste.

₱40.00

Sulu Coffee (Kapeng Sulu or Kahawa Sug)

A traditional coffee from Sulu, Mindanao, made from Robusta beans.Typically served strong and black.A staple drink in the Tausug community, often paired with native delicacies like Bangbang Sug.

₱50.00

Kalinga Coffee

Grown in the Kalinga province in the Cordilleras, mainly from Robusta and Arabica beans.Has a bold and slightly bitter taste with a hint of nuttiness.Traditionally brewed in a clay pot over fire.

₱70.00

Excelsa Coffee

A rare coffee variety also grown in some parts of Luzon and Mindanao.Known for its unique, fruity, and tart flavor with a mild bitterness.Often blended with other coffee types to enhance complexity.

₱80.00

Mount Apo Coffee

Grown in the fertile lands of Mt. Apo, Mindanao.A high-quality Arabica coffee with a smooth, chocolatey, and slightly acidic taste.One of the most expensive Philippine coffee varieties.

₱90.00

Musang Coffee (Civet Coffee or Kape Alamid)

Made from coffee beans that have been eaten and fermented in the digestive system of civet cats before being collected.Known for its smooth, less acidic, and chocolatey taste.One of the rarest and most expensive coffees in the world.

₱100.00
